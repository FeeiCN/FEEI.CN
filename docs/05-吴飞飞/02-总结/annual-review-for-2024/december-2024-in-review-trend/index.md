## Do the Best at Work

**CY2024 Work Summary and Plan** . In the past month, I have spent a lot of time summarizing and planning this year’s work for teams at all levels. I don’t want to talk too much about the specifics, after all, doing well in such matters is no longer a challenge for me. Focusing on doing a specific task, I have overlooked crucial aspects like trends, opportunities, impacts, and my future.

**What are the potential career advancement opportunities for a CISO** ? During a private CISO conference in Shenzhen, Guangzhou, I raised the question for discussion. Based on my observations, there are no CISOs in China who have become CTOs or CEOs (excluding those who have started their own companies). However, other technical areas like R&D, algorithms, and products often see people advancing to CEO positions at significant rates. Can a CISO from a small company grow into a CISO at a large company? Can a CISO from a large company advance to a broader security role, such as a CRO? I didn’t get an answer, everyone is focused on their own challenges.

**Jack Ma Spoke at Ant Group’s 20th Anniversary** . December 8th is Ant Group’s 20th anniversary, and the company organized an event to celebrate it. Jack Ma, who had not appeared publicly for many years, came out to share his insights with us. Over the past 20 years, we have created Alipay, Yu’e Bao, and Ant Forest out of nothing, and today he is here for the next 20 years.**In the next 20 years, the massive changes driven by the AI era will exceed everyone’s imagination. AI will be an even greater era, changing everything but not determining everything. We need to create truly valuable and unique things for this upcoming age.**

- At this critical moment, the company made numerous management adjustments. The CEO of Alipay was promoted to President of Ant Group, a CFO from Cainiao Network was brought in, the digital payment and digital connectivity businesses were separated, and adjustments were made for the CTO position.

**Outstanding new graduates are offered very high salaries**. Recently, I discussed compensation for new graduates and learned about offers from other companies. An outstanding incoming master’s graduate in information security can earn a monthly salary of RMB 40k, not including signing bonuses and year-end bonuses. While not everyone or every company can offer such a package, it still surprised me. Keep in mind that even highly experienced and excellent professionals who have been working for years may not earn that much, and it took me six years to reach that level. Trends matter a lot: a rapidly growing business can offer higher salaries, faster promotions, larger teams, more resources, and better business development, creating a positive cycle.

**Get anonymous labels** from coworkers about you. Recently, in a gathering with my manager and my team, we played this game. Each person drew a self-portrait and signed it on a piece of paper. With eyes closed, others wrote tags on the paper. Finally, they had to choose one tag that best described them and another that did not fit them at all and guess who wrote each tag. This game helped everyone refresh their impressions of each other and significantly increased mutual understanding within the team.

- I’m very confident about Tesla’s future and have made multiple profitable trades on it, making it one of my best long positions. Even after an 80% increase, I was convinced that there would continue to be positive developments before and even after Trump took office, allowing me to hold onto the stock. However, I still allowed others’ opinions to influence my trading decisions.
- The main problem is that there’s no unity between what**I trust and how I act** . When making decisions, I haven’t given it much thought, and my attitude towards the matter isn’t clear or consistent enough. This lack of commitment will only result in mediocrity in this area.
- My income is decent enough that my need for money isn’t urgent, which has led to a lack of motivation to pursue other ways of earning. However, having more money would allow me to achieve my dreams earlier and do more of what I want. It could also improve the quality of life for my family.
- Generate profits in fields where you excel, and within a limited timeframe, concentrate on optimizing your performance in these profitable areas. Avoid switching tracks randomly and limit experiments to unfamiliar territories to a minimum.

**Personal Pension Fund** . This year, the national rollout of individual pension plans started, and to understand public sentiment towards them, I conducted a small survey within my connections. A total of 57 participants were surveyed, and salary was not the most important factor influencing. their decision. Different choices primarily depended on whether they had an optimistic or pessimistic outlook.

- 44 people (77%) did not use or do not plan to use it due to:
  - Long payout period for funds received (considering retirement planning too early/severe inflation).
  - High uncertainty regarding future outcomes (concerns about the pension fund deficit and principal losses / whether they will live that long).
  - It is viewed as a cost rather than a benefit (little tax refund, withdrawal involves taxes, feels like donating money).
- 18 people(18%) used it mainly because:
  - High tax refunds offer high effective yields, allowing for investment growth (especially in A-share index funds).
  - The total amount involved is not significant.
  - They believe in the future and see potential inheritance benefits.
- 3 people (5%) were undecided or still researching due to historical losses from past investments and lack of clarity regarding pros and cons.
